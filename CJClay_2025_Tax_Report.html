A First-Time Penalty Abatement (FTA) request or a reasonable cause argument may be available given: (1) CJ had a professional advisor who failed to inform him, (2) he has been otherwise compliant (2024 payment plan current, no prior 1120-S delinquencies), and (3) the termination was abrupt and in bad faith.
