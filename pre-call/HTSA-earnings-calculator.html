Industry basics
How to read this model
Offer size $8k–$12k avg. for HTSA members · $5k–$25k broad range · $25k+ usually experienced closers
Commission ~90% of companies pay 10%–15% (“accelerator”) — e.g. 40%+ close rate → 15%
Show rate Industry standard 75% — 8 booked ≈ 6 conversations
Close rate Lower 20% · Good 25% · Great 35%+ · Best 40–50%
Payment mix Most offers: PIF + 4-pay (e.g. $10k PIF or $12k on 4-pay — 4×$3k/mo)
Schedule Full-time closers often 35–40+ hrs/week · Industry-average mix ≈ 65% PIF/financing · 35% 4-pay

PIF vs. 4-pay slider: This model keeps 4-pay on the same offer price as PIF (conservative). In the real world, 4-pay is often priced higher (example: $12k on 4-pay vs. $10k PIF). More 4-pay = more residual stacking in the year-one chart below — not more upfront cash.
