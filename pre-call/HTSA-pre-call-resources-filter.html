1

Average offer size (deal value)

Remote high-ticket offers often fall in a roughly $5k–$20k band; many land $8k–$12k. We default $5k to stay conservative — tabs are rounded; your partner may price differently (e.g. PIF vs a slightly higher multi-pay).

2

Close rate (conversation → sale)

Industry bands are wide: many see ~15–25% as a common “average” range on qualified conversations; stronger reps on warm inbound often land ~25–35%; cold or messy traffic can be much lower. New to the seat — we default 20%.

3

Your commission on money collected

Full commissions in this space are often about 10–15% of what the buyer pays; some partners go higher as you prove out. We default 10% so the sketch stays conservative.
