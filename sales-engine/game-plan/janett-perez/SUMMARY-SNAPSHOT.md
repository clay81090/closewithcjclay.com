## Building Urgency (the point of the call)

Urgency comes from the **gap + time + emotion**, not from pressure. Three levers:

1. **The money gap** — quantify it. A $4k–$8k/month gap over a year is **$48k–$96k left on the table.**
2. **The time cost** — "Another year figuring it out alone is another year at the current number."
3. **The ceiling** — most "sales professionals" are capped by their **offer and comp plan**, not their skill. HTS = uncapped, remote, higher ticket.

**Reframe line:**
> "You don't have a skill problem, Janett. You have an **access problem** — you're selling the wrong offer for the income you want. That's fixable fast."
