## Summary Snapshot

Jada is a **healthcare professional** exploring a **career move into sales** because of **income pressure**. She is not coming from a sales background, but she self-identifies as **hard-working, coachable, people-oriented, and fully committed**.

This is a **transition story**, not a leverage optimization story (contrast: Colton Haskin). The emotional driver is **financial stress + desire for a better career path**, not burnout from a job she otherwise loves.

### The cleanest read

**She needs a higher-income vehicle that matches her work ethic and people skills — and she needs a structured path because she cannot afford to guess.**

### What she likely wants (discover on call)

- More monthly income / financial stability
- A career with upside, not just another hourly healthcare grind
- Remote or flexible work (assume — confirm)
- A real plan, not motivational content

### What we know she has

- Healthcare experience (patient-facing, high trust, high pressure)
- Strong self-reported connector / people skills
- Willingness to learn and "110%" dedication language
- Motivation tied to real income pain

### What we don't know yet (must discover)

- Exact role in healthcare (RN, CNA, MA, admin, etc.)
- Current take-home income
- Target monthly income number
- Family / spouse / dependents
- Debt or urgency level
- Sales familiarity (high-ticket vs generic sales)
- Schedule bandwidth for training
- Payment capacity ($3k vs $6k)

- Pick **Setter or Closer** and commit
