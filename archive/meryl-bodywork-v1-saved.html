Meet Meryl

Hi, I'm Meryl.

I grew up in Johnson County, Kansas, where I spent twelve years as a competitive swimmer before deciding to see what life looked like beyond the pool. That decision led me to Pittsburg State University, where I earned my degree in Physical Education with an emphasis in strength and conditioning, graduating with a 4.0 in my major along with minors in psychology and biology.

Today I live in Springfield, Missouri, where I stay active through CrossFit and continue learning about strength, movement, and nutrition. My goal is simple: to help people take care of their machine, body and mind, inside and out. I believe a strong, efficient body paired with a strong, positive outlook is one of the biggest keys to a good life, and massage therapy is one of the most powerful tools I have to help people get there.
